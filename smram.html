Microbiome Data Description

Data Composition

Our microbiome data include 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing data and metagenomic sequencing data of human skin microbiome samples retrieved from Sequence Read Archives (SRA), as well as 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing data of respiratory tract microbiome samples.
